To use our Scrabble word finder:
  1. Input the letters in your hand into the box that says “Enter your letters here…”.
  2. Enter in letters on the board that are optimal for making a word in the box that says “Enter open characters on the board…”. For example, for the board shown below, "N", "T", "M", and "E" are all open characters. "I" and "H" could also be considered open, but "A" is not open because any word using it would also have to form words horizontally because it would touch the letters in "NIGHT".
  3. Click the button that says SEARCH NOW to get a list of potential Scrabble you could play, sorted by score in descending order.
