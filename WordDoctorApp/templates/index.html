Wordle

Players have six attempts to guess a five-letter word; feedback is given in the form of colored tiles, indicating when letters match or occupy the correct position.

Scrabble

Scrabble is a word game in which two to four players score points by placing tiles, each bearing a single letter, onto a game board divided into a 15x15 grid of squares.

Anagrams

Anagrams (also known as Anagram, Snatch, and Word Making and Taking) is a tile-based word game that involves rearranging letter tiles to form words.

Boggle

Boggle (also known as WordHunt) is a word game played using a plastic grid of lettered dice, in which players look for words in sequences of adjacent letters.

WordScapes

Wordscapes is a word puzzle video game where the player must find every word using the letters that are located in the circle at the bottom of the screen.
